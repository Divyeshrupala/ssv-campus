Welcome To SHREE VARDAYINI HIGH SCHOOL

At Shree Vardayini High School, We Believe In Fostering A Vibrant And Inclusive Community Where Students Can Thrive Academically, Socially, And Personally.

Shree Vardayini High School Is A Co-Educational School Affiliated With The GSEB Board, Offering Education In Both English And Gujarati Mediums Across Two Shifts. The School's Goal Is To Provide Holistic Education, Emphasizing Excellence In Both Academic And Co-Curricular Activities. With Its Tagline "ज्ञान की जड़ें, सपनों के पंख", The School Aims To Support Students From Rural Areas Through Quality Education. Pedagogy Forms The Basis Of Teaching At Shree Vardayini High School, Focusing On Improving Learning Quality, Student Receptiveness, Participation, Effective Knowledge Impartation, And The Development Of Higher Cognitive Skills Among Students.

At SHREE VARDAYINI HIGH SCHOOL we aim at having a learner-centric quality education and all-round development of the students at affordable cost. We develop qualities of body, mind and character which will enable the students to become good and productive citizens of the country. To become a centre of excellence by empowering the inherent potential of every learner to achieve their goals by providing multi-dimensional holistic education and development in scholastic and co-scholastic areas.
