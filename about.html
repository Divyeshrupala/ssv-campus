ABOUT SSV Campus

"A NEW HOPE"

SSV CAMPUS is a 10+2 co-educational school offering education in both English and Gujarati medium that started its humble journey in 2018, located in sector 3/B, Gandhinagar. The school is affiliated with the Gujarat State Education Board (GSEB). SSV CAMPUS has emerged as a well-known English Medium school, focusing on the all-round development of its students. Our school has a spacious and ventilated classroom equipped with the most modern amenities and a relatively big playground. School infrastructure comprises of spacious assembly hall, science lab, computer lab, CCTV Camera and school library Sports Facilities and adequate sports facilities are available in the school. It has one Multi Purpose Ground including one Football ground, one Basketball Courts, one Volleyball Court, two (Outdoor) Badminton Courts, and a Skating Rink. The school has a well-established art cum activity room. The school is fully equipped with a well-planned, child-friendly and safety-enriched infrastructure that goes a long way in ensuring the holistic development of the students in curricular as well as co-curricular arenas.

The well-qualified and experienced teachers manage the teaching-learning system. At SSV CAMPUS, your child is about to begin one of the most exciting stages in their life, and every faculty member is committed to making it an enriching experience for them. SSV CAMPUS believes that academic education alone is not enough to be successful in life and hence it caters to the harmonious development of the children through co-curriculum activities organized throughout the year.
       Our school believes in producing refined, civilized and cultured human beings who can live with dignity and honour. Overall SSV CAMPUS stands on par with the best schools in Gandhinagar in terms of infrastructure, academic excellence, sports and discipline.



WHY CHOOSE US?

SSV Campus focuses on providing a well-rounded education, emphasizing not only academic excellence but also the development of co-curricular skills and character.

With its tagline "A New Hope," SSV Campus is committed to providing support, resources, and opportunities to students facing challenges, fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment.

Being affiliated with the Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board ensures that the curriculum aligns with state educational standards, providing students with a recognized and reputable academic program.

SSV Campus utilizes pedagogy-based teaching methods aimed at improving learning outcomes, student participation, and the development of higher-order thinking skills.

Offering education in both English and Gujarati mediums accommodates students from diverse linguistic backgrounds, enhancing inclusivity and accessibility.
