MISSION

Our Mission is to empower all students to apply their acquired skill & knowledge, and rely upon their personal attributes to lead productive lives & to become contributing members of the global community. Our mission is to provide a high quality comprehensive & meaningful education for all students.

Vision

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate & value knowledge & skills that will support them, as life long learners to participate in & contribute to the global world & practice the borne values of the school : Respect to laurence & inclusion & excellence.
